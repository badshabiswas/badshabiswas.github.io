Welcome to Md. Badsha Biswas!

Md. Badsha Biswas is currently pursuing his PhD in Computer Science at George Mason University, VA, USA. Before starting his Ph.D., he worked as a Software Engineer at BJIT, a global software development and IT services company, created as a joint enterprise between Japanese and Bangladeshi entities. He received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science & Engineering from University of Chittagong, Bangladesh in August, 2022.
Md. Badsha Biswas has been very passionate about research from the very beginning. His research interests aren't limited to Bio-informatics, Natural Language Processing, Software Engineering, Human-Computer Interaction, and Cyber Security. Md. Badsha Biswas has already done a poster presentation on the field of NLP for the Bengali language titled "Coreference Resolution for Bangla".

Previously He worked under the supervision of Dr. Abu Nowshed Chy on TREC-IS which is based on machine learning algorithm for tracking information streams, an undergraduate dissertation in the fulfillment of B.Sc degree. Md.Badsha Biswas's broad research focuses on Bio Informatics, Natural Language Processing, Artificial intelligence, and Machine Learning. He is pollyannaish of making a beautiful world with Science and technology.
