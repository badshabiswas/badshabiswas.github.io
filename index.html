Welcome to Md. Badsha Biswas!

This is Md. Badsha Biswas , a student of Computer Science & Engineering at University of Chittagong, Bangladesh where he has been pursuing his undergraduate since 2017.
Md. Badsha Biswas is very much passionate about Artificial intelligence from the very beginning. His research interests are Data Science (Data Mining & Analytics, Information Retrieval; Bio-informatics, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning Techniques, Robotics & Human-Computer Interaction, Cloud & Distributed Computing, Industry 4.0 (IoT, Pervasive Computing, Cyber Physical System), Computer Vison, Semantic Web. Md. Badsha Biswas have already done a poster presentation on the field of Natural Language Processing for Bengali language titled "Coreference resolution for Bangla". Now He is working under the supervision of Dr. Abu Nowshed Chy on TREC-IS and within a short period of time will publish a paper on incident stream track system.

Md.Badsha Biswas broad research focus on Artificial Neural Network, Data Science, Deep Learning. Currently he is working on some projects related to artificial intelligence. He is pollyannaish of making a beautiful world with Science and technology.
