Welcome to Md. Badsha Biswas!

His research interests aren't limited to Data Science (Data Mining & Analytics, Information Retrieval; Bio-informatics, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning Techniques, Robotics & Human-Computer Interaction, Cloud & Distributed Computing, Industry 4.0 (IoT, Pervasive Computing, Cyber Physical System), Computer Vison, Semantic Web.
